Sacred Rituals

🔥

Agni Sanskar

The sacred fire ceremony that purifies and sanctifies the union, witnessed by the divine fire as your eternal witness.

Key Steps: Fire preparation, sacred mantras, seven circles around Agni, offering prayers for prosperity and happiness.
👑

Mangalsutra Dharana

The sacred thread that binds two souls, representing protection, love, and the eternal bond of marriage.

Key Steps: Blessing the mangalsutra, chanting protective mantras, placing around bride's neck with love and devotion.
🦶

Saptapadi

Seven sacred steps representing seven lifelong promises, each step taking you closer to eternal unity.

Key Steps: Seven vows for food, strength, prosperity, family, progeny, seasons, and friendship - binding for seven lifetimes.
🌾

Kanyadaan

The sacred giving away of the bride, representing the transfer of responsibility and blessing of new beginnings.

Key Steps: Father's blessing, sacred water ceremony, joining of hands, prayers for the couple's prosperous future.
💍

Sindoor Daan

The sacred vermillion ceremony marking the bride as a married woman, blessed with protection and prosperity.

Key Steps: Blessing the sindoor, applying with ring finger, chanting protective mantras, family blessings.
